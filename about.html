About Me

Currently, I am pursuing my PhD in Economics at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor. I graduated in 2016 from Vassar College. Prior to beginning my PhD, I spent three years at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania as a research assistant.

My research interests lie at the intersection of behavioral economics, labor economics, and public economics.
